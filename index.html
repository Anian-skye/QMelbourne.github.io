The amount of published data and findings makes it impossible for a human to survey the entirety of relevant publications exhaustively which inherently leads to the question of what kind of important information or insight may go unnoticed or insufficiently appreciated.(公布的数据和发现的数量使得人们无法彻底地调查整个相关文献集，这本质上导致了什么样的重要信息或洞察力可能被忽视或不充分理解的问题。)

The complex and highly heterogeneous nature of data of interest requires a sufficiently nuanced analytical framework which is capable of inferring the wide range of changes and interactions between topics over time（感兴趣的数据的复杂和高度异构性质需要一个足够细微的分析框架，能够推断出主题之间的广泛变化和相互作用。）

This information can provide valuable insight into the refinement of ideas and findings in the scientific com- munity, effected by new research and accumulating evidence.（通过新的研究和积累的证据，这些信息可以为科学界的思想和发现的完善提供有价值的见解）

Despite T-SNE is able to preserve many interesting qualities of the semantic space, projecting the entire keyword space into the same display makes the appreciation of details in proximity data a harder task to achieve, even if the appro- priate interaction techniques are correctly applied.

P2V: large‐scale academic paper embedding

goals: Learn high-quality embeddings for academic papers on large-scale datasets.

contributions:

  1. a model that can learn high-quality paper embeddings from text and citation links.
  2. scale to large dataset.
  3. Has state-of-the-art performance on paper clas- sification, paper similarity and influence prediction tasks.

methods:

  1. three types of relations when learn- ing paper embeddings: word–word, document–word, and document–document relations.

  2. Model : Three Layer Shallow Neural Network

visualization:

Temporal Quasi-Semantic Visualization and Exploration of Large Scientific Publication Corpora

goals: The tool allows the user to search for particular topics, track their temporal interdependencies (e.g. ances- tral or descendent topics), and examine their domi- nance within the corpus across time.

Modelling topic evolution over time ：

Key idea: While topics may change significantly over time, changes between successive epochs are limited.

Incremental embedding for temporal networks

goals: Introduce ICMEN the first-in-class incremental meta-embedding method that produces vector representations of nodes respecting temporal dependencies in the graph.
